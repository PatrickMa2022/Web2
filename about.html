About Me

Who Am I?

I am a student pursuing a bachelor's degree in Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering Technology, along with a minor in Business, at Texas A&M. I am currently a sophomore in the Corps of Cadets, and am also a member of the Club Swim Team and the Corps of Cadets Marathon Team. Prior to college, I attended Jesuit College Preparatory School in Dallas, Texas, and Windermere Preparatory School in Windermere, Florida.

Throughout high school, I participated in swimming, robotics, tennis, soccer, and band. I also enjoy playing sports, hiking, going to the beach, running, playing the trumpet and the piano, as well as learning about cars.
